Membership Rewards Spirit

As an American Express® Qantas Corporate Platinum Card Member, you’re automatically enrolled in the Membership Rewards1 Spirit program.

View Card details

Earning points

  • American Express Membership Rewards enrolment is complimentary for American Express® Qantas Corporate Platinum Card Members1.
  • Earn 2 Membership Rewards points for every $1 spent on your Card on Qantas products and services, with no points cap1.
  • Earn up to 1.5 points2 per $1 spent on your Card up to 225,000 points per year and up to 1 point per $1 spent after that on purchases (except those listed below).
  • Earn 0.5 Membership Rewards points per $1 spent on utilities, telecommunications, insurance (except those issued by American Express) and 0.25 points for all government bodies3.
  • There’s no limit to the number of Membership Rewards points you can earn and they don’t expire4.

Redeeming points

  • Transfer your points to Qantas Points at a rate of 1:1 and redeem for flights, upgrades or products from the Qantas Store5.
  • Shop at your favourite stores with an extensive range of Gift Card Partners6.
  • Shop online with points for your favourite brands at David Jones7.

1. Membership Rewards enrolment required (an annual fee may apply). Includes the following Qantas Products and Services purchased on the Qantas merchant account (i.e. directly from Qantas): Qantas passenger flights (with a QF flight number), Qantas Holidays, Qantas Branded non-airfare products, and Qantas Frequent Flyer and Qantas Club membership joining and annual fees. Subject to the terms and conditions of the Membership Rewards program available here. Your Company must allow participation in Membership Rewards to be eligible to enrol. You must enrol yourself and cannot delegate someone else in the Company to do so on your behalf. There may be tax implications associated with participation in the Membership Rewards program. You are advised to check with your accountant or tax adviser for further information.

2. American Express Qantas Corporate Platinum Card Members enjoy an accelerated earn rate of up to 1.5 Membership Rewards points per dollar spent up to 225,000 points per annum, reverting to up to 1 point for every dollar spent thereafter.

3. You will earn 0.5 Membership Rewards points per $1 spent at participating merchants classified as "utilities" including gas, water and electricity providers; “telecommunications”; "insurance" excluding insurances offered by American Express and 0.25 points per $1 spent at merchants classified as "government" including the Australian Taxation Office, the Australian Postal Corporation, federal/state and local government bodies. Exceptions may apply if an individual merchant does not accept American Express as a form of payment.

4. As long as your Car Account remains current and in good standing.

5. Qantas Frequent Flyer members: You must be a Qantas Frequent Flyer member to earn and redeem Qantas Points. The redemption of Qantas Points is subject to the Qantas Frequent Flyer Program terms and conditions available at qantas.com/terms. A joining fee may apply. We recommend you consult your accountant or tax adviser to ensure you understand possible tax implications, for example fringe benefits tax (if applicable).

6. Gift Card or voucher rewards are not redeemable or exchangeable for cash or credit and are valid for a minimum period of three months from the date of issue, unless otherwise stated. Normal retailer Gift Card and voucher conditions apply. Minimum redemption amounts apply.

7. Subject to the terms and conditions of the Membership Rewards program available here and the David Jones terms and conditions available at DAVIDJONES.COM.AU. Only American Express Cards issued by American Express Australia Limited are eligible provided they are enrolled in the Membership Rewards program. Excludes Supplementary Card Members. A minimum of 3,000 points must be redeemed at any one time. If redeeming with Points + Pay, the 'pay' component of your purchase will be automatically billed to your American Express Card Account and will appear on the first billing statement following redemption. You’ll earn Membership Rewards points on the ‘pay’ component of your purchase only.
