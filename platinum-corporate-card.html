Maximise your points earning

By using an American Express Corporate Card for business payments, you could earn points on purchases like fuel, utilities, car hire, travel, rent, taxes and more5.

Best of all, Membership Rewards points never expire and there’s no limit to the number of points you could earn6.

Enjoy a range of exciting rewards

Like to treat yourself? Want to treat your family? Why not do both? Membership Rewards offers the flexibility to redeem how you want. So whether it’s a family trip or an in-store Gift Card, the choice is yours.

About your Membership Rewards program

American Express® Corporate Platinum Card Members are eligible to enrol in the Membership Rewards5 Ascent program.

Earning points

  • Earn 1 point per $1 spent on purchases, except those listed below.
  • Earn up to 0.5 points per $1 spent on utilities, insurance (except those issued by American Express) and 0.25 points for every dollar spent with government bodies7.
  • There’s no limit on the points you can earn and they never expire6.

Redeeming points

  • Use points for air travel and hotel stays8.
  • Shop at your favourite stores with an extensive range of Gift Card Partners9.
  • Use points to shop for your favourite brands at David Jones online10.
  • Transfer your points to 9 Frequent Flyer Programs and 2 Hotel Frequent Guest Programs11.

Protection when you travel

Things don’t always go to plan when traveling – occasionally luggage gets lost, flights get delayed or missed, and injury or unforeseen illness result in medical expenses4.

Our Inconvenience Cover, Medical Emergency Expense Cover or Travel Inconvenience cover could help you out of a tricky situation4.

Card replacement worldwide

If your Card is lost or stolen we can usually get a replacement Card to you, no matter where in the world you are. Best of all, we can normally get it there within 1 to 2 business days, and at no cost12.

Unwind before take-off at the American Express Lounge

Relax before a business trip or holiday at the Sydney International Airport American Express Lounge13. Simply show this Card to enjoy complimentary access including free Wi-Fi, charging stations and complimentary trolleys.

Relax at 1,000 airport lounges worldwide

With Priority Pass lounge access at over 1,000 airports worldwide you’ll be able to escape the bustle, enjoy some refreshments and access free Wi-Fi (where available) while you're waiting to board14.

Baggage, Money and Documents Cover

On business trips, it's good to know that you're covered if your baggage is lost, damaged or stolen. Cover is automatic when you pay for your return trip with this Card, and provides you with reimbursement for, or replacement of, your baggage, money or documents4.

Let Platinum Travel Service take care of business

Our team of experts can help with many aspects of your domestic or international itinerary 24 hours a day, 7 days a week – including booking flights, hotels, meeting spaces and restaurants15.

Assistance when you need it most

Wherever in the world you are, Emergency Global Assist® ensures 24/7 help is on hand for things like legal and medical emergencies, emergency cash and lost luggage or passports16.

Platinum Concierge at your service

Whether travelling for business or holidaying with the family, the Platinum Concierge is just a phone call away.

They can help 24/7 with requests including dinner reservations, flight changes or tickets to events2.

Relax in style before the show

Spoil staff, clients or yourself with the star treatment at our American Express Invites® Lounges in Qudos Bank Arena or the Brisbane Entertainment Centre. Your Card gets you waiter service to your table, complimentary Wi-Fi and loan of portable phone chargers17.

Live it up with American Express Invites®

Entertain staff and clients with memorable dining experiences, pre-sale concert tickets and invitation-only events.

Plus, explore Taste: serving up-and-coming dining hotspots and hidden gems. Card Members can pick from 400 specially selected restaurants around the world18. Members even get something on the house. It could be 20% off the bill, a free appetiser or a complimentary bottle of wine.

Fine Dining: your table's waiting

Looking for the perfect dining experience for business associates or friends? Fine Dining's got you covered19.

You can secure a table at partner establishments in Australia and around the world.

Enjoy extras on every stay

Whether you're travelling for business or holidaying with the family, be treated to exceptional benefits at some of the world's most stylish hotels20.

Stays come with privileges such as early check-in, room upgrades, daily breakfast for two, late checkout, in-room Wi-Fi and complimentary massage or food vouchers.

Save on car rental with Avis

Enjoy savings and other benefits with the Avis Preferred Membership, including car upgrades and 25% off standard rates (or 2.5% off promotional rates)21.

Complimentary news access

Stay on top of the latest business news with a subscription to The Sydney Morning Herald, The Age or The Australian Financial Review22.

Online Fraud Protection Guarantee

If your Card is used for unauthorised purchases, you won’t be held responsible. As long as our Security Centre team are contacted straight away, they’ll investigate and arrange for the amount to be credited back to your Account as well as send a replacement Card23.

Fast, secure and convenient mobile payments

Fast, secure and convenient mobile payments American Express is proud to offer eligible Card Members fast, easy and convenient ways to make mobile payments with Apple Pay, Samsung Pay and Android Pay24.

Extend your cash flow by up to 51 days

If you are currently paying expenses by cash, invoice or cheque, the payment will leave your account right away. With the American Express Corporate Platinum Card, so long as you pay your balance in full each month, you have up to 51 days until your payment is due, which means you can keep money in your Account for longer and have more flexibility when managing your finances3.


Automate your bookkeeping with ExpenseIt®

Receive near real-time alerts on your mobile device when you make a business purchase with this Card. You could also take a photo of your receipts to automatically generate pre-populated expense reports25.

Manage your Card on the move

See transactions, check charges and make payments on your mobile or any other device even when you're travelling. Staying on top of your Card activity with the American Express mobile app couldn't be simpler.
