Turn Business spend into rich rewards

As a Business ExplorerTM Credit Card Member, you are automatically enrolled in the Membership Rewards Gateway program and can earn Membership Reward points on all purchases. When you consider the points earning potential, it makes a lot of sense to use your Card every time you spend for business.


  • Earn 2 Membership Rewards points for every $1 dollar spent on all purchases except government bodies in Australia, where you earn 0.5 points per $1 spent3.
  • There is no points cap on the amount of points that you can earn or the rate at which you can earn them. Your points won’t expire as long as your Account is in good standing.
  • Earn Rewards faster on purchases your employees make with employee Cards for your business at no additional costs5.

Business Loyalty Bonus - 50,000 Bonus Points annually

Let your business expenses reward you every year. Spend $100,000 every 12 months from your Card approval date and receive 50,000 Membership Rewards Bonus Points4. That’s enough for two return flights from Sydney to Melbourne6.

Using Points

You can redeem your points for a wide range of rewards: • Transfer your points to a choice of 8 airline partner programs7 – Asia Miles™, Emirates Skywards, Etihad Guest, Malaysian Airlines Enrich, Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer, THAI Royal Orchid Plus, Velocity Frequent Flyer and Air New Zealand Airpoints™. Choose to use your points to pay eligible charges on your statement with Select + Pay with points8.

Complimentary Travel Insurance

When you travel domestically and internationally for your business, you will have peace of mind knowing that you’ll have extensive protection. It covers you, your partner, any dependent children under the age of 22 and any employees who have a Supplementary Card when the Card is used to pay for your business travel9.

Access cover for incidents such as:

  • Damaged, destroyed, lost or stolen personal baggage, money and documents during your trip.
  • Medical emergency expenses.
  • Delayed flight or missed flight connection.
  • Luggage delay for luggage checked on a scheduled flight.
  • Travel disruption due to the unforeseen and unavoidable cancellation of a trip.

Start your journey in style

Make your business travel more relaxing with two entries to the American Express Lounge at Sydney International Airport T1 per calendar year10.

Relax in style before the show

Give staff or clients the star treatment at our American Express Invites Lounges at Qudos Bank Arena, Perth Arena or the Brisbane Entertainment Centre.


You'll get waiter service to your table, complimentary Wi-Fi and loan of portable phone chargers. Plus, receive a complimentary bottle of water when you spend $15 on your American Express Business Explorer™ Credit Card11.

Cash flow management

Support your cash flow with up to 55 days interest free12 on purchases when your balance is paid in full every month.

Order additional Cards for employees

Earn rewards faster, while keeping track of your staff expenses with up to 99 Supplementary Cards at no extra cost. That way you'll earn Membership Rewards points whenever you and your Supplementary Card Members spend5.

Save time with transaction data feeds

American Express provides transaction data feeds to MYOB accounting software, helping you better manage your business expenses, save time and increase productivity13.


Card Members must be enrolled with MYOB for automatic transaction data feed to take place. For more information and to enrol your American Express Card®, please visit myob.com.au/bank-feeds

Embrace greater efficiency, access and control

American Express AccessLineTM is a smarter way to pay. Settle payments with your domestic and international suppliers using your American Express® Business ExplorerTM Credit Card, even if they don’t accept American Express Card payments14.

Added convenience of Mobile Wallets

Use Apple Pay, Samsung Pay or Android Pay mobile wallets to pay for purchases. Simply load your Card to the relevant app on your eligible mobile device and use it to pay15.

Tax deductable Card Membership fees

Your annual Card Membership fees may be tax deductable when your Card is used solely for business purposes16.

Emergency Card Replacement

With our global network of offices, we can usually get a replacement Card to you virtually anywhere in the world within 48 hours17.
