Maximise your points earning

By using your American Express® Business Accelerator Credit Card for business payments, you could earn points on payments – like fuel, accommodation, utilities, car hire, travel, rent, taxes and more2.

Your Membership Rewards points won’t expire and there’s no limit to the number of points you can earn5.

Choose your rewards

Like to treat yourself? Like to invest your points back into your company? Why not do both.

Membership Rewards offers the flexibility to redeem how you choose. So whether it’s a family trip or using your points to reduce your Card balance6, the choice is yours.

About your program

American Express® Business Accelerator Credit Card Members are eligible to enrol in the Membership Rewards2 Ascent program.


Earning points

  • Enjoy an accelerated earn rate of 2 points per $1 spent up to 100,000 points per year. You'll earn 1 point per $1 spent after that2.
  • Earn 1 point per $1 spent on purchases, except those listed below2.
  • Earn 0.5 points per $1 spent on utilities, insurance (except those issued by American Express) and all government bodies7.
  • There’s no limit to the points you can earn and they don’t expire4.

Redeeming points

  • Use points for air travel and hotel stays8.
  • Shop at your favourite stores with an extensive range of Gift Card Partners9.
  • Shop with points at David Jones10 and Ticketmaster11.
  • Transfer your points to 9 Frequent Flyer Programs and 2 Hotel Frequent Guest Programs12.

Card replacement worldwide

If your Card is lost or stolen we can usually get a replacement Card to you, no matter where in the world you are. Best of all, we can normally get it there within 1 to 2 business days, and at no cost13.

Relax in style before the show

Spoil staff, clients or yourself with the star treatment at our American Express Invites® Lounges in Qudos Bank Arena or the Brisbane Entertainment Centre. You’ll enjoy waiter service to your table, complimentary Wi-Fi and loan of portable phone chargers14.

Order additional Supplementary Cards

Give your key staff a card and earn rewards faster while keeping track of your expenses4.

Online Fraud Protection Guarantee

If your Card is used for unauthorised purchases, you won’t be held responsible. As long as our Security Centre team are contacted straight away, they’ll investigate and arrange for the amount to be credited back to your account as well as send a replacement Card15.

Save time with MYOB Integration

American Express provides transaction data feeds to MYOB accounting software, ensuring a more time efficient and seamless business experience16.

If you're enrolled with MYOB, transactions on an American Express Card can be transferred via an automatic feed, for quick and easy reconciliation.

Fast, secure and convenient mobile payments

American Express is proud to offer eligible Card Members fast, easy and convenient ways to make mobile payments with Apple Pay, Samsung Pay and Android Pay17.
